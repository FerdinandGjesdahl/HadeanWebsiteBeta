Our Story

A European partner for life science innovation

Hadean Ventures is a European life science venture capital fund manager with approximately EUR 230 million of assets under management. The firm invests across all healthcare verticals and stages in products with the potential to address unmet medical needs.

The stated purpose of the firm is to make a difference by investing in the future of healthcare — supporting research, development, and the teams behind tomorrow's breakthrough treatments.
