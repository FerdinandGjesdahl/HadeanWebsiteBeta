ESG Integration

Responsible investing = value creation

Hadean Ventures contributes to increased wealth, better health, and improved living conditions across the globe through support of research and development — with a strong focus on environmental, social, and governance matters.

We welcome the Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation (SFDR), which links the UN's 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and the Paris Agreement to the investment decisions of financial institutions. These regulations make finance a powerful tool for creating overall value.

Our investment due-diligence process has been tailored to meet high ESG standards. Through board directorships and impactful governance positions, we implement ESG policies and standard operating procedures across our portfolio companies.

Diversity & Inclusion

Driving change in the industry

Lack of diversity and gender equality is a major challenge in the investment industry and start-up community. Hadean Ventures works for an inclusive and diverse environment within its firm and portfolio companies, promoting equal opportunity in all recruitment processes.

Programs are in place to help increase diversity both in start-ups and in investment roles. We participate in cross-firm, cross-border initiatives such as Level 20 — which we helped co-launch in the Nordics in 2019 together with several other private equity firms.
