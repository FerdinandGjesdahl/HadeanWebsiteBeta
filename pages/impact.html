ESG & Responsible Investing

The Hadean Impact

Hadean Ventures has a strong commitment to being a driver for positive changes in society as a whole — through the companies we invest in and the way we invest.

Our Approach

Responsible investing equals value creation

The firm contributes to increased wealth, better health and improved living conditions across the globe through support of research and development and with a strong focus on ESG matters.

⚖️

SFDR Compliance

Hadean Ventures welcomes the Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation (SFDR), which links the UN's 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and the Paris Agreement on climate change to the due-diligence processes and investment decisions of financial institutions. These regulations make finance a powerful tool for creating overall value.

🔬

ESG in Due Diligence

The investment due-diligence process has been tailored to meet high ESG standards, and technical standards continue to be implemented. Through impactful positions such as board directorships, Hadean Ventures uses its influence to implement ESG policies and standard operating procedures in portfolio companies.

🌱

Sustainability Goals

The UN Sustainable Development Goals directly impacted by the firm include good health and well-being, gender equality, industry and innovation, and responsible consumption and production. The launch of the SDGs in 2015 underscored the role of the private sector in solving global challenges.

Diversity & Inclusion

Strong focus on diversity and gender equality

The firm's ESG policy guides its consideration of environmental, social and governance factors within both the investment firm itself and the companies it invests in. Lack of diversity and gender equality is identified as a major challenge in the investment industry and start-up community.

Hadean Ventures works for an inclusive and diverse environment within its firm and portfolio companies and promotes diversity and equal opportunity in recruitment processes.

Programs are in place to help increase diversity both in start-ups and in investment roles. Hadean Ventures participates in cross-firm, cross-border initiatives such as Level20, which it helped launch in the Nordics in 2019 together with several other private-equity firms.

⚡

Level20 — Nordic Launch

In 2019, Hadean Ventures co-founded Level20 in the Nordics — a cross-firm initiative dedicated to increasing female representation in private equity to 20% and beyond.

A pan-European initiative, Level20 brings together leading private equity firms committed to improving gender diversity across the industry.
