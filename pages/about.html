About Hadean Ventures

Where transformative science begins

European life science venture capital — backing breakthrough healthcare from origin to impact.

Our Story

Built on science. Driven by purpose.

Named after the Hadean eon — Earth's earliest geological period, when the conditions for life first emerged — Hadean Ventures was founded to leverage the vast, untapped potential of European life science innovation. We invest at the origin of transformative medicine.

With approximately €230 million of assets under management across two funds, we invest across all healthcare verticals and stages in products with the potential to address unmet medical needs. Therapeutics, diagnostics, medical devices, digital health — if the science is exceptional and the need is real, we engage.

From our offices in Oslo and Stockholm, we partner with extraordinary entrepreneurs and scientific founders across Europe and beyond — providing capital, operational expertise, and strategic guidance to accelerate the journey from discovery to patient impact.

Our Approach

How we invest

Rigorous scientific due diligence combined with active, hands-on partnership at every stage of the journey.

01

Identify

We source opportunities across all healthcare verticals — therapeutics, diagnostics, digital health, and medical devices — with a focus on European innovation that carries global potential. Our network spans academic institutions, clinical centres, and the broader venture ecosystem.

02

Evaluate & Partner

Deep scientific and clinical due diligence, leveraging our team's medical and life science expertise to assess the science, the market, and the path to value creation. We take board seats and work alongside management from day one.

03

Scale

We support portfolio companies through clinical milestones, regulatory approvals, commercial launches, and strategic exits — providing operational guidance and capital to drive step-changes in value for patients, founders, and investors.

Our Investors

Backed by leading institutions

Hadean Ventures funds are supported by leading European and US-based institutional and private investors, as well as the government-backed fund investors Saminvest AB, Argentum, and Investinor.

Our investor base includes fund-of-funds, pension funds, family offices, and foundations — a diversified, committed group of partners who share our long-term vision for healthcare innovation.
