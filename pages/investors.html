Our LP Base

A trusted network of institutional and private capital

Our funds — Hadean Capital I and Hadean Capital II — are backed by a carefully selected group of leading European and US-based investors who share our commitment to transformative healthcare innovation and responsible investing.

Our investor base includes institutional investors, family offices, pension funds, foundations, and government-backed investors. Government-backed fund investors include Saminvest AB, Argentum, and Investinor.

In December 2023, Hadean Ventures announced the oversubscribed final close of Hadean Capital II — a testament to the confidence our LP network places in our team and strategy.
