Tell us about your venture

We invest across healthcare verticals and stages, from early-stage science to growth-stage companies. If you are building something that could transform patient outcomes, we would like to hear about it.

Hadean Ventures is particularly interested in opportunities with strong scientific foundations, differentiated approaches, and clear paths to clinical and commercial impact. We back companies across Europe and beyond.
